### Is Your Neural Network at Risk? The Pitfall of Adaptive Gradient Optimizers, 2024

This paper has an interesting observation on the model robustness based on the type of gradient optimizer used in training a model. Their extensive empirical and theoretical analysis shows that models trained with SGD optimizer have high robustness against adversarial perturbatioins than adaptive optimizers like RMSProp and ADAM.
